# ctrlcsv 

**ctrlcsv** is a minimal, single-page utility designed to instantly turn pasted text into a `.csv` file. Built for researchers, students, and AI power users who need to bridge the gap between LLM outputs and structured data without compromising privacy.

## 🚀 Quick Start
1. Open `ctrlcsv.html` in any browser (mobile or desktop).
2. Paste your raw data (Comma, Tab, or Semicolon delimited).
3. Click **Save to File**.

## 🔒 Why ctrlcsv?
* **100% Private:** Data is processed entirely in your device's memory. No servers, no tracking, and no internet access required after the first load.
* **Zero Friction:** No accounts, no "Pro" tiers, and no setup. It is a single-file tool that stays out of your way.
* **Auto-Detection:** Intelligently identifies your delimiter as you type.
